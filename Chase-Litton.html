My Take

Chase Litton never showed much statistically in college. The team that drafts him will do so because of his size, age, and arm strength. He is essentially a poor man's Josh Allen without the mobility. He's not a lock to be drafted, but shows up more in full mock drafts than some that were statistically superior.
